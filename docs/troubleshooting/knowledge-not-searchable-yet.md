---
title: Document not searchable yet
---

## Symptoms

- A document appears in Knowledge, but search returns no results.

## Why it happens

Documents may require time to ingest and index before they become searchable.

## What to do

1. Wait a few minutes.
2. In **Knowledge**, select **Refresh**.
3. Try your search again.

## If the issue persists

- Confirm the document status is not “failed”.
- Capture the trace ID shown on the search panel (if present) and contact your administrator.
